Stellaris AI

Stellaris Ai is a web application that converts complex academic PDFs into simplified, structured notes to help students study more effectively and reduce academic dishonesty. I personally designed and developed the full-stack application, implemented the PDF parsing system, integrated an AI model for summarization and simplification, and built the user interface. I also trained and refined prompt engineering to generate student-friendly explanations. Technologies used: Python, JavaScript, GeminiAPI, PDF parsing libraries, HTML/CSS.
